What are parrots?

What makes these birds parrots?

  • Curved Beaks
  • Zygodactl feet (Four toes on each foot. Two toes that point forward, two toes that point backward.)

    What are the pros of owning a parrot?

  • Have a longer life span than most pets
  • Can increase your amount of social interaction
  • Some parrots can talk
  • Variety of colors and sizes

    What are cons of owning a parrot?

  • Parrots are messy
  • Not all can talk
  • Some sizes of parrots can make them incompatible
  • Need more attention than most pets
  • Long lifespan means not a lot of travelling
